ABOUT ME

To be honest, I'd rather be eating cheetos and doing TV show marathons rather than typing this. But according to my university's career advisors, collecting Game of Thrones trivia and knowing where to find discounts on potato chips are not skill sets that employers are looking for (who knew). So, I created this website to show the world that even a couch potato like moi is capable of being a productive member of society. On a more serious note, I am a 3rd year student at the University of British Columbia (UBC) doing a combined major in Business and Computer Science. On this site you'll find a link to my blog, some projects I've worked on, my work history, and my contacts.
